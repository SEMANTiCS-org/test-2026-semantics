# This was SEMANTiCS 2025!
##### September 06, 2025 by SEMANTiCS

The **SEMANTiCS 2025** Conference took place in Vienna, Austria, this September — once again bringing together the global community of experts in semantic technologies, data management, and artificial intelligence.
Researchers, industry leaders, and innovators from all over the world joined to discuss the latest advancements in knowledge graphs, explainable AI, and the interplay between symbolic and sub-symbolic technologies.

Among this year’s highlights were inspiring industrial presentations by ZEISS and IKEA.
ZEISS demonstrated their Service Copilot powered by an industrial knowledge graph and agent-based orchestration, while IKEA showcased their enterprise-wide knowledge graph transformation focusing on sustainability and consumer awareness.
These examples showed how the combination of structured knowledge and large language models can lead to more intelligent and trustworthy systems.

Key topics of SEMANTiCS 2025 included Trustworthy AI, Data Governance, and Digital Sustainability — reflecting the growing importance of transparency, responsibility, and ethics in AI development.

With more than 300 participants from academia and industry, SEMANTiCS 2025 once again served as a vibrant hub for knowledge exchange, innovation, and collaboration.
The event reaffirmed that the future of artificial intelligence lies at the intersection of meaning, data, and trust.
